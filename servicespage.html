Survey Division

The Geoscience Division provides services and products associated with collecting, processing, interpreting, managing, and storing geological and geophysical data. These datasets are used for evaluating the presence of natural resources, including oil, gas, water, and minerals, and for optimizing the exploration, appraisal, development, and production of those resources. Clients are oil and gas companies, mining companies, and governmental organizations. Our division also has the expertise to create geological deposit models which can be used to help with resource estimation and development planning.
